## Prompt

A rectangle has length 12 and width 7. What is its area?

### Base

A rectangle has length 12 and width 7. What is its area? To find the area of a rectangle, you multiply its length by its width.

Given:
- Length = 12
- Width = 7

Area = Length × Width
Area = 12 × 7
Area = 84

So, the area of the rectangle is 84 square units.

### SFT-LoRA
